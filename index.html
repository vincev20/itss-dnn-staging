Testimonials

See how IDEA sparks joy, creativity, and real results for students and professors. These stories show the magic of learning with a playful twist!

MONTHON SORAKRAIKITIKUL, Ph.D

"I had the privilege of piloting the ITSS(Innovative Thinking System for Students) workshop at Thammasat University, and the results exceeded expectations. Students actively generated ideas, created their own evaluation criteria, and collaborated with peers to refine their projects. This hands-on process allowed them to simultaneously develop creativity and logical reasoning.

From the students’ reflections, many shared how their self-perceptions had shifted: “I thought I wasn’t creative, but I found confidence through the techniques.” — “The safe atmosphere encouraged me to contribute.” — “Ideas grew much stronger when developed together with peers.” These voices demonstrate that ITSS is not just about brainstorming methods, but about cultivating students’ independence and fostering a mindset of continuous creative growth.

I strongly recommend ITSS as an outstanding educational program that empowers students to think differently, collaborate effectively, and transform their ideas into practical outcomes. I sincerely hope it will be adopted more widely across Thailand and internationally."
